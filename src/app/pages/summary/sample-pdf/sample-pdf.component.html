PAYMENT CURRENCY: Tanzania Shillings

JURISDICTION: Tanzania

WEATHER INDEX INSURANCE

  • Germination failure due to deficit rainfall at germination stage
  • Growth failure due to deficit rainfall at vegetative stage
  • Flowering failure due to deficit rainfall at flowering stage
  • Pre-harvest damage due to excessive rainfall

As defined in Annex 1. Insurer shall not pay for any event outside these definitions.

INSURED PERILS: Deficit/excess rainfall based on satellite index data

EFFECTIVE COVER INCEPTION DATE: As defined in Annex 1

FARM LOCATION: GPS coordinates of farmer locations

EARLIEST POSSIBLE CONTRACT START: 1st November, 2025

WEATHER DATA SOURCE

ARC2 by NOAA (USA). Free at: ftp://ftp.cpc.ncep.noaa.gov/fews/AFR_CLIM/ARC2/

Missing data will be filled using historical averages.
